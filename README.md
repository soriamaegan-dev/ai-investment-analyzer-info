## What Does It Do?

The AI Investment Analyzer is the most comprehensive free investment research tool available on HuggingFace. It combines stock analysis, cryptocurrency analysis, prediction market bidding analysis, portfolio management, real-time news impact, scenario building, contrarian detection, fear and greed analysis, and an events calendar — all in one free app powered by Qwen 72B AI. This is the ONLY free tool that combines all of these features in one place.

---

## 10 Powerful Tools in One App

### 📈 Stock Analyzer
Enter any stock ticker and the AI delivers a complete 9-section professional analysis covering company overview, market sentiment, fundamental analysis, technical analysis, current events impact, buy/hold/sell recommendation with target price and stop loss, position P&L analysis, risk assessment, and key events to watch.

### ₿ Crypto Analyzer
Enter any cryptocurrency and the AI delivers a complete 9-section analysis covering market sentiment, token economics, market cycle phase, support and resistance levels, regulatory impact, buy/hold/sell recommendation with DCA strategy, position P&L analysis, and crypto-specific on-chain alerts.

### 📊 Bidding Analysis
Select your prediction market platform and the AI analyzes any contract or market question. Supports Polymarket, Kalshi, PredictIt, Manifold Markets, Metaculus, Robinhood Prediction Markets, and any other platform. Delivers true probability assessment, edge calculation, Kelly Criterion bet sizing, platform-specific considerations, and a complete buy/sell/hold recommendation.

### 💼 Portfolio Dashboard
Enter your stock holdings, crypto holdings, and prediction market positions and the AI delivers a complete portfolio analysis including total value and P&L, asset allocation, risk scoring, diversification assessment, position-by-position recommendations, rebalancing actions, and risk management alerts — with an interactive allocation chart.

### 📰 News Impact Engine
Paste any breaking news headline and the AI instantly identifies which stocks go up, which go down, how crypto is affected, which prediction market contracts change probability, what to do with your specific holdings, and delivers a 30-minute action plan — including a contrarian view on whether the market reaction is an overreaction.

### 🎭 Scenario Builder
Describe any hypothetical scenario and the AI stress tests your portfolio against it. Delivers bull, bear, and base case outcomes with probability estimates, portfolio impact in each scenario, specific stocks and crypto to buy before it happens, positions to hedge, and prediction market contracts that become valuable.

### 🧠 Contrarian Detector
Enter any asset and the AI analyzes where the crowd may be wrong. Delivers a crowd sentiment score, data vs narrative comparison, historical contrarian precedents, a contrarian opportunity score out of 10, and a specific contrarian recommendation with entry timing and sizing guidance.

### 😱 Fear & Greed
Describe current market conditions and your emotional state and the AI calculates a Fear and Greed score across stocks, crypto, and prediction markets. Delivers the Warren Buffett signal, asset-specific recommendations, an action plan for the current score level, and a contrarian checklist to prevent emotional decision making.

### ⏰ Events Calendar
Select a lookout period and the AI generates a complete upcoming events calendar covering economic releases, central bank meetings, major earnings, crypto events, political and regulatory events, and prediction market resolutions — with pre-event positioning strategy, cash deployment plan, and a weekly action checklist.

### 💬 AI Investment Chat
Ask anything about investing in plain English. Get analysis, explanations, SQL queries, Python code, and deeper insights on demand. Conversation history maintained throughout your session. Covers stocks, crypto, prediction markets, portfolio strategy, macroeconomics, and behavioral finance.
